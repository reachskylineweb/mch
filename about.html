About Madras Coffee House

Brewing Tradition, Serving Excellence

Our Journey

With over 15 years of experience and a network of 186 operating outlets, Madras Coffee House has established a strong presence across India. What began as a mission to serve genuine South Indian filter coffee has evolved into a scalable and trusted retail brand that combines tradition with modern operational excellence. Our kiosks are designed to deliver fast service, consistent quality, and memorable customer experiences in high-footfall locations.

What Makes Us Different

Our signature filter coffee is crafted using carefully sourced premium coffee beans from select estates, expertly blended, roasted, and ground to achieve the perfect taste. By following traditional decoction brewing methods and standardized preparation processes, we ensure that every cup delivers the authentic flavor that our customers love.

We combine this authentic coffee experience with a complete support ecosystem designed to ensure QSR excellence at every location.

  • ☕ Premium-quality ingredients and sourcing standards
  • 🛡️ Strict quality control and food safety practices
  • 📋 SOP-driven operations for consistency
  • ⚡ Quick-service formats designed for convenience
  • 🥟 A diverse menu of hot beverages, cold beverages, and snacks
  • 🤝 Continuous training and support for our teams

Our Commitment to Quality

Quality is at the heart of everything we do. From vendor selection and ingredient sourcing to preparation and service, every stage follows a structured quality assurance framework. We maintain stringent standards through regular audits, batch-wise quality checks, food safety protocols, and compliance-driven processes to ensure that every product served reflects the Madras Coffee House promise.

Our Impact

Today, Madras Coffee House proudly serves customers through a growing network of outlets supported by trained professionals and successful franchise partners. Our continued expansion is driven by strong customer loyalty, operational efficiency, and a commitment to delivering authentic South Indian flavors to every location we serve.

By empowering local entrepreneurs and establishing a standardized supply chain, we create sustainable job opportunities and standard operational excellence across India, while keeping the rich heritage of filter coffee alive and accessible.

👁️ Our Vision

To become India's most trusted and accessible destination for authentic South Indian coffee and beverages by creating memorable experiences, empowering entrepreneurs, and delivering consistent value to customers, partners, and communities.

🎯 Our Mission

To preserve the tradition of authentic South Indian filter coffee while building a modern, scalable retail ecosystem that offers exceptional quality, operational excellence, and sustainable growth for all stakeholders.
